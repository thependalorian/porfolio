Buffr - Financial Inclusion Platform

Winner: 2023 Asper Student Startup Prize

Revolutionizing African Digital Payments

As the electronic payments market in Africa experiences rapid growth through financial technology innovation, Buffr aims to transform financial transactions in Namibia and beyond.

"We will vertically integrate into a payment gateway and expand across Southern Africa and developing countries."

- George Nekwaya, MBA'24

The Challenge

  • High reliance on cash transactions
  • Expensive electronic payment alternatives
  • Limited financial access in rural areas

Our Solution

  • Secure digital payment platform
  • Low-cost transaction model
  • Gradual expansion across Southern Africa
