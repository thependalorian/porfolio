The Hero's Journey

Origins in Namibia

The journey began in Namibia, where a foundation in engineering was established at the Namibia University of Science & Technology. This provided the initial training grounds for developing problem-solving abilities and technical knowledge.

The Call to Adventure

Seeking to expand horizons beyond engineering, the hero ventured into the realm of business and data analytics, recognizing the power of combining technical expertise with strategic thinking.

Crossing Borders

The quest led to the United States, specifically to Brandeis International Business School, where new skills in data analytics, strategy, and innovation were acquired. This marked the transformation from engineer to strategist.

Trials and Victories

Through internships and projects, various challenges were overcome, from analyzing banking data at Insait IO to developing career platforms for clean tech companies. Each quest provided valuable experience and increased skill levels.

The Return With Elixir

Armed with knowledge from both technical and business realms, the hero now combines these powers to create sustainable solutions in clean energy and water management, making meaningful impact on the world.
